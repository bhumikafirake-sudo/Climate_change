What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns. It is primarily driven by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial emissions that increase greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.

Main Causes

  • 🔥 Burning of fossil fuels (coal, oil, gas)
  • 🌲 Deforestation and land use changes
  • 🏭 Industrial and agricultural emissions
  • 🚗 Transportation and urbanization

Solutions

  • 🌱 Transition to renewable energy (solar, wind, hydro)
  • 🚲 Promote sustainable transport (EVs, public transit)
  • 🌳 Afforestation and reforestation
  • ♻️ Reduce waste and adopt circular economy practices
